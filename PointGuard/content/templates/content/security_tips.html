Important Security Tips

Use a Unique Password for Each Account

Do not reuse passwords across multiple sites. Each password should be unique and used for one account only.

Choose Strong Passwords

Create passwords that are at least 12 characters long and include a mix of numbers, upper and lower case letters, and symbols.

Update Passwords Regularly

Change your passwords periodically to reduce the risk of exposure and breach.

Use Two-Factor Authentication

Whenever possible, enable two-factor authentication (2FA) for an additional layer of security beyond just a password.

Beware of Phishing Attacks

Do not click on links or open attachments in unsolicited emails. Be wary of emails that request your password or personal information.
